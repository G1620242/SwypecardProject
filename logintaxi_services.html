Taxi Services

Below will be an indepth description on how to pay for services work and what you will have to do to use the card correctly when you wish to travel in a taxi.

To pay for a taxi you will need to book from a taxi company and give your location and destination to them. The company will send the nearest taxi to you however this is reliant on the taxi company and does not incorporate us until you pay for your service.

When the taxi arrives and takes you to your location you will be charged based on how far the car has driven you will pay by swiping the card in the scanner that is in the taxi, this will pay your fare at the end of your journey without the need for change making your payment smoother and allowing for a better experience with this mode of transportation.

Some taxi companies do not work with us and therefore will not use the swypecard machine therefore before you start the jorney make sure you can pay to avoid any trouble.
