Bus Services

Below will be an indepth description on how the bus services work and what you will have to do to use the card correctly when you wish to travel on buses.

Buses with our company will be fitted with a card scanner that allows you to easily swipe your card over it to pay for your journey. Since the money is prepaid there is nothing else you have to do other than enjoy your journey as the system will have recognised that you have paid successfully notifying the bus driver.

Journeys that may require multiple buses will work the same way and you will have to use the card for both journeys. This will count as two payments as you will be using different buses.

Swypecard is working with many bus companies around the U.K so there is many methods to get around using the card and many places that you can get to using the service that is provided with the companies we work with.
