Train Services

Below will be an indepth description on how the train services work and what you will have to do to use the card correcly when you wish to travel from train stations

To pay for a train journey you will need to get a ticket by using your card on the scanner that will allow you to buy a ticket for the train which you can use to leave and re enter the station and used to board the train itself.

The price for the tickets vary on the length of the journey and how many trains it will take howver the money will be pre paid for when you use the card to buy the tickets .

Boarding trains has been made easier with swype card as it can be used as a card to get you through the gate and onto your journey without the hassle of struggling to get a ticket.
